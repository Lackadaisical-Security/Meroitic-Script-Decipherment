    = canonical dedication verb-object-recipient structure. Then di + snṯr (incense,

    materials of Kushite identity — gold = wealth/divine, iron = industrial/military.

    claim. se (conf 1.0) + [parent name] = genealogical validation. The inscription
    immediately establishes WHO she is and WHERE she comes from.

    carrying the full king title. Not unusual in Meroitic — it's expected. This directly
    contradicts any subordination reading: she IS king of Kush by title, not 'queen of'.
  
  ye imnt (conf 0.98): THE canonical funerary formula, ~28 attestations. LS framework:
    FIXED idiomatic phrase, not compositional. ye = go/journey (conf 0.70, context-
    dependent) + imnt = west/West (conf 0.85, funerary symbolic). The combination is
    formulaic and should be translated as a unit: 'the westward transition has been made'.
    Do NOT expand to metaphysical doctrine beyond what the formula itself states.
  
  FOUR-DEITY DIVINE WATER SEQUENCE: amn + ꜣpd-mk + wos + sorey — all with identical
    structure: [deity] di ato n [name of deceased]. This is the INVERTED form of the
    dedication formula — in temples (di ato n [deity]), the human gives to the god.
    In funerary texts, the god gives to the human. The grammar reverses, the formula
    mirrors. ato (sacred water, conf 0.85) as consciousness-flow medium: the deceased
    needs consciousness sustenance, the gods provide it.
  
  DEITY SELECTION ANALYSIS: amn (Egyptian, conf 0.98) + ꜣpd-mk (indigenous Kushite,
    conf 0.80) + wos = Isis (conf 0.60) + sorey = Osiris (conf 0.60). Two confirmed
    high-confidence + two lower-confidence. The Egyptian pair Isis/Osiris in funerary
    context is expected (Osirian resurrection theology); the Kushite pair Amun/Apedemak
    is indigenous. Together: theological synthesis, not Egyptian subordination.
  
  TRIPLE ETERNITY SEAL: ḏt × 3 (conf 0.92 each) = maximum permanence encoding. Compare
    Royal inscription above (ḏt × 2). The extra seal in funerary context = the dead
    require MORE permanence assertion than the living — identity must be locked harder
    once the person can no longer speak their own name aloud.
  
  IDENTITY MANTRA CLOSE: kdi kdi kdi terminates = Kush claims the deceased. She died
    AS Kush. The mantra is both personal identity and national continuity assertion.

  ══ STRUCTURAL ANALYSIS ══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

  DEITY-FIRST ORDERING: ꜣpd-mk nb tꜣ opens BEFORE the royal title = deity precedence
    in this temple. nb (lord/master, conf 0.92) + tꜣ (land/territory, conf 0.80) =
    compound epithet 'lord of the land' — site-specific to Musawwarat, not universal.
    At Meroe and Napata, Amun typically leads. Here, Apedemak leads: the temple's
    patron determines the inscription's opening deity.
  
  ALTERNATING DEDICATION PATTERN (critical finding):
    Offering A → ꜣpd-mk: snṯr (incense, aromatic/sensory offering)
    Offering B → amn:     nbw (gold, precious metal)
    Offering A → ꜣpd-mk: ꜣbw (ivory, animal-derived luxury)
    Offering B → amn:     hbny (ebony, rare black timber)
    Pattern: ꜣpd-mk receives NATURAL/ANIMAL products; amn receives REFINED/TRADED goods.
    This is a domain division: Apedemak = nature/earth domain; Amun = civilization/wealth.
    The king performs the same verb (di) for each — the differentiation is in the OBJECT.
  
  MATERIAL GOODS DENSITY: nbw (conf 0.94) + ꜣbw (conf 0.95) + hbny (conf 0.90) + snṯr
    (conf 0.90) = all 4 items are high-confidence and all 4 are Kush's primary exports.
    What is offered to the gods IS what Kush sells to the world. Trade goods = sacred
    goods. The economy and the theology share the same material vocabulary.
  
  ato di n kdi (REVERSED FORMULA): Standard = di ato n [deity] = human gives water to
    god. Here = ato di n kdi = sacred water is given to/for Kush = the LAND is the
    recipient. The temple itself becomes the medium: through the dedication ceremony,
    divine water flows not to a specific person but to the nation as collective entity.
    ato acts as the transfer medium between cosmic and national identity.
  
  MINIMAL SEALING: Only ḏt × 1 (contrast: Royal = ×2, Funerary = ×3). Temple
    dedications are ACTIVE, ongoing. They don't need permanence locked as hard as
    funerary inscriptions. The temple continues operating; the deceased does not.

  ══ STRUCTURAL ANALYSIS ══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

  ACCOUNTABILITY STRUCTURE: sš (scribe, conf 0.82) + [name] + se (conf 1.0) + [parent]
    = professional identity + genealogical verification. The scribe is IDENTIFIED —
    not anonymous. This is an accountability document, not just a shipping manifest.
    Someone answers for this record. The se particle here is administrative, not
    genealogical — though the form is identical. Multi-domain particle reuse.
  
  swty (merchant, conf 0.82): Separate professional title from sš — confirms a
    distinct MERCHANT CLASS in Kushite society. The scribe records, the merchant
    transacts. Two professional roles, two titles, one document.
  
  ḫꜣrw (caravan, conf 0.78) + napata se mroe: se reused as spatial 'originating from'
    connector. Napata = origin point (LS lexicon: conf 0.95); mroe = dispatch city
    (conf 0.95). The caravan assembled at Napata but was officially dispatched from
    the capital Meroe — administrative distinction between assembly and official origin.
  
  WEIGHT STANDARD ANALYSIS:
    dbn (deben, conf 0.85) = standard weight unit (~90g copper equivalent in Egyptian
    system, likely adapted in Kush). nbw (gold, conf 0.94) × 10 dbn = ~900g gold.
    ꜣbw (ivory, conf 0.95) × 3 dbn = ~270g = small carved ivory parcel, not raw tusks.
    hbny (ebony, conf 0.90) + snṯr (incense, conf 0.90) = bulk goods, no weight listed
    = likely priced by volume/bundle not by unit weight.
  
  ROUTE NETWORK VOCABULARY:
    wꜣt (road/route, conf 0.90) = base route marker
    wꜣt-tꜣ-mḥw = [road]-[land]-[north/Lower Egypt] = formal name for Nile-to-Egypt road
    wꜣt-ḥꜣpy-rs  = [road]-[Nile flood]-[south/upper] = Upper Nile corridor
    swn (Aswan, conf 0.88) + rꜣ-qd (Alexandria, conf 0.88) = northern axis terminals
    ꜣksm (Axum, conf 0.88) + ꜣrb (Arabia, conf 0.88) = eastern/Red Sea axis
    FOUR named infrastructure points = Kush operated formal route cartography.
  
  šnʿ (trade/barter, conf 0.75): Verb used with both Aswan AND Alexandria — confirms
    BOTH cities were active trade nodes, not just transit stops. Kush was exchanging
    at both the Egyptian frontier (Aswan) and the Mediterranean hub (Alexandria).
  
  SEASONAL BRACKETING: šmw (harvest/drought season, conf 0.80) + ꜣḫt (inundation
    season, conf 0.80) = temporal boundary for the journey. Caravan departs in dry
    harvest season (travel easier), expected return by inundation (high Nile waters
    could disrupt river travel). Agricultural calendar = operational calendar.
  
  NO DEITY REFERENCES: Confirms LS framework finding — commerce = secular domain.
    The same goods (nbw, ꜣbw, hbny, snṯr) appear in temple dedications WITH deity
    formulas. Here: zero formulas. The script code-switches between sacred and secular
    registers. Same vocabulary, different register.

  ✦ UNIVERSAL 'se' PARTICLE
    Appears across ALL FOUR types:
      • Royal:   qore se [name]       = heir/descendant of
      • Funerary: kndke se [name]     = heir/descendant of
      • Temple:  qore se [name]       = heir/descendant of
      • Admin:   sš [name] se [name]  = scribe son of
                 ḫꜣrw [city] se [city] = caravan originating from
      → se is the MORPHOLOGICAL BACKBONE of Meroitic, functioning as
        genealogical connector, spatial origin marker, AND apposition
        linker. Single particle, multi-domain. (conf 1.0)

  ✦ ato — SACRED WATER AS UNIVERSAL MEDIUM
    Present in Royal, Funerary, Temple (absent Administrative = secular):
      • Royal:    di ato n [deity]  = human → god  (offering direction)
      • Funerary: [deity] di ato n [person] = god → deceased (reversed)
      • Temple:   ato di n kdi      = water → land (national scale)
      → Three distinct formula directions, same medium (ato). Sacred water
        is the transfer mechanism between ALL cosmic entities in Meroitic
        theology. The grammar direction encodes the RELATIONSHIP, not just
        the action. (conf 0.85)

  ✦ MATERIAL GOODS OVERLAP: TRADE = SACRED
    nbw / ꜣbw / hbny / snṯr appear in BOTH Temple dedication AND Administrative:
      Temple:  offered to deities as divine gifts
      Admin:   shipped between cities as commodities
      → The SAME objects bridge the sacred and secular registers. Kush's
        economy and theology were not separate systems. What you trade is
        what you offer to the gods. The ledger and the altar speak the
        same vocabulary.

  ✦ kdi × 3 vs ḏt × 3 — TWO IDENTITY MODES
    kdi × 3:  Royal + Temple = ACTIVE identity assertion
               'We ARE Kush, right now, in this ceremony'
    ḏt × 3:   Funerary maximum seal = PRESERVED identity assertion
               'This person REMAINS Kush, even in death'
    ḏt × 2:   Royal standard close = 'this act is permanent'
    ḏt × 1:   Temple = 'this dedication stands'
      → The count is not decorative. It encodes the INTENSITY of the
        permanence claim. Funerary = hardest lock (death requires it).
        Administrative = zero (commerce is transient by nature).

  ✦ SECULAR DOMAIN MARKER: ABSENCE OF DEITIES
    Royal → amn + ꜣpd-mk present
    Funerary → amn + ꜣpd-mk + wos + sorey (FOUR deities)
    Temple → ꜣpd-mk + amn (deity-primary ordering)
    Admin → ZERO deity references
      → The Meroitic administrative register is COMPLETELY secular. Same
        script, same writers, same goods vocabulary — but the moment you
        enter commerce, the divine formulas vanish. This is code-switching
        at the register level, not two different traditions.
